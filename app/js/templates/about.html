Inspired Web Developer

Born and raised in Orange County, CA, I am a work hard, play hard individual. Dissatisfied with the lack of space for expression and creativity, as well as change, within the medical field, I looked elsewhere to feed the caged beast of passion inside of me.

I stumbled upon programming in the early summers of 2014 and began to encompass my life around self-motivating and self-teaching myself how to code. Once I hit a wall and couldn't find any more online resources, I took my talents to Seattle, WA in order to surpass this hurdle and was accepted into the Code Fellows full-stack Javascript accelerator.

Graduating from Code Fellows in December 2014, I am now proficient in JavaScript, Node.js, AngularJS, Bootstrap, MongoDB, Express, HTML/CSS and much more. However, my thirst for learning has not yet been quenched and I continue to practice and learn upcoming changes to the programming world. For example, I have set my sights on learning React/Flux and the upcoming changes in ES6 and Angular 2.0 .

Currently, I am looking for an environment where I can not only refine my craft but also thrive under the fast-paced pressures of development and work with a team that I can benefit from and return the favor. I hope to remain in Seattle to live and breathe coding culture for a little while longer as well as explore an area I am unaccustomed to.

In my spare time I like to go hiking, try different foods and beers/whiskys, hunt the web for new music, and spend an excessive amount of time on fashion, e-Sports, and gaming. Coffee is foreign to me, but I would love to get into it and I make a pretty decent guacomole. Other than that, I love to code and explore everything that's new in the rapidly changing world around me.
